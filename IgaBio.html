Biography

 

Iga Świątek is a Polish professional tennis player and one of the most dominant forces in women's tennis. Born on May 31, 2001, in Warsaw, Poland, she turned professional in 2016 and quickly made history by winning her first Grand Slam title at the French Open in 2020 at just 19 years old.

Since then, Świątek has established herself as a multi-time Grand Slam champion and a consistent World No. 1 on the WTA Tour. Her game is built on incredible footwork, heavy topspin groundstrokes, and unmatched mental toughness. Known for her aggressive baseline play and dominance on clay, she has also proven her ability to win on hard courts, making her one of the most complete players of her generation. Świątek has already secured her place among the sport’s greats and continues to push for more dominance. As one of the most well-rounded and mentally tough players on the WTA Tour, she is expected to add many more Grand Slams to her name in the years to come.
